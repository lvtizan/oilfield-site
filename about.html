我们是谁Who we are

把合适的动力与装备，交付到您的现场。Delivering the right power and equipment to your site.

KST 集团成立于 2005 年，是一家多元化工业集团，核心业务涵盖矿山、专用车辆、工程机械、发电与油气钻采。集团扎根重工业与能源装备领域，依托专业技术与全球供应链，为客户交付可靠的产品与一体化解决方案。 Founded in 2005, KST Group is a diversified industrial conglomerate with core businesses spanning mining, special vehicles, construction machinery, power generation and oil & gas drilling. Rooted in the heavy-industry and energy-equipment sectors, the Group draws on professional technology and a global supply chain to deliver reliable products and integrated solutions.

集团与卡特彼勒（Caterpillar）、SJPETRO、别拉斯（BELAZ）、康明斯（Cummins）、博杜安（Baudouin）、斯坦福（STAMFORD）等世界领先企业保持长期深度合作，凭借稳定的品质与高效的服务，赢得国内外市场的认可。 The Group has maintained long-term, in-depth cooperation with world-leading enterprises — including Caterpillar, SJPETRO, BELAZ, Cummins, Baudouin and STAMFORD — and has earned recognition across domestic and overseas markets for stable quality and efficient service.

面向海外客户，KST POWER 聚焦发电与油田钻采装备——发电机组、工业发动机，以及钻井 / 修井 / 固井 / 压裂成套设备——提供按型号选型、参数确认与快速报价，全程中英文对接。 For overseas customers, KST POWER focuses on power generation and oil & gas equipment — generator sets, industrial engines, and complete drilling, workover, cementing and fracturing packages — with model-based selection, specification review and fast quotation in both English and Chinese.
