公司简介Company

我们是谁Who we are

把合适的动力与装备，交付到您的现场。Delivering the right power and equipment to your site.

KST POWER 专注三大产品线——发电机组、工业发动机、油田钻采成套设备，覆盖发动机 74–4400 hp、钻机钻深至 9000 m、工作压力至 175 MPa，广泛应用于油气井场、矿山与电力现场。 KST POWER focuses on three product lines — generator sets, industrial engines and complete oilfield drilling & production equipment — covering engines from 74 to 4400 hp, drilling rigs to 9000 m, and working pressure to 175 MPa, for oil & gas wellsites, mining and power sites.

我们依据井深、工况、功率与排放要求为客户匹配型号，提供成套供货、参数确认与快速报价，全程中英文对接，让海外客户也能顺畅选型与沟通交期。 We match models to well depth, duty, power and emissions requirements, providing complete-package supply, specification review and fast quotation — with bilingual communication so overseas customers can select and coordinate delivery smoothly.

我们把工作聚焦在选型的确定性上：清晰的型号与参数、可对比的技术表、明确的交期与报价——让每一次采购都少走弯路。 We focus on certainty in selection: clear models and specifications, comparable spec tables, and definite lead times and pricing — so every purchase takes the shortest path.
