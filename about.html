我们是谁Who we are

代理商的价值，在于消除距离。A distributor's value is to remove distance.

广州市泽康松特机械设备有限公司是 SJ 石油四机（SJPETRO · SINOPEC EQUIPMENT）产品的授权代理商。我们的角色，是让远在中国的世界级产能、原厂工程能力与全球现场经验，成为您触手可及的资源。 Guangzhou KST Mechanical Equipment Co., Ltd is an authorized distributor of SJ Petroleum Machinery (SJPETRO · SINOPEC EQUIPMENT) products. Our role is to make world-class capacity, factory engineering and global field experience — based in China — a resource within your reach.

石油四机始建于 1941 年，坐落于中国湖北荆州，拥有约 82 年的钻采装备制造积淀，是中国石油钻采装备行业的骨干企业，也是中国钻井、修井、固井、压裂装备出口第一的制造商。 Established in 1941 in Jingzhou, Hubei, China, SJPETRO brings roughly 82 years of oilfield drilling-and-production equipment manufacturing. It is a backbone enterprise of China's petroleum drilling-and-production equipment industry, and the country's leading exporter of drilling, workover, cementing and fracturing equipment.

凭借遍布 俄罗斯、乌兹别克斯坦、科威特、阿尔及利亚、阿曼、印度尼西亚 六个国家的海外机构，原厂产品已出口至 40 多个国家。我们把这张网络作为交付与售后的支点，让选型、供货与服务都在离井场更近的地方发生。 Through overseas offices across Russia, Uzbekistan, Kuwait, Algeria, Oman and Indonesia — six countries in all — original-factory products already reach more than 40 countries. We use that network as the pivot for delivery and after-sales, so selection, supply and service happen closer to your wellsite.

我们提供 原厂品质与质保、原厂选型建议、成套供货、经六国机构网络的本地化售后，以及全程中英文销售支持——一个界面，对接大厂全部资源。 We provide original-factory quality and warranty, factory selection advice, complete-package supply, localized after-sales via the six-country office network, and end-to-end bilingual sales support — one interface to the full resources of a major manufacturer.
