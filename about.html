公司简介Company Profile

KST 集团KST Group

KST 集团成立于 2005 年，是一家多元化工业集团，核心业务涵盖矿山、专用车辆、工程机械、发电与油气钻采。集团扎根重工业与能源装备领域，依托专业技术与全球供应链，为客户交付可靠的产品与一体化解决方案。 Founded in 2005, KST Group is a diversified industrial conglomerate with core businesses covering mining, special vehicles, construction machinery, power generation, and oil & gas drilling. Rooted in heavy industry and energy equipment sectors, the Group relies on professional technologies and a global supply chain to deliver reliable products and integrated solutions to customers.

集团与卡特彼勒（Caterpillar）、SJPETRO、别拉斯（BELAZ）、康明斯（Cummins）、博杜安（Baudouin）、斯坦福（STAMFORD）等世界领先企业保持长期深度合作，凭借稳定的品质与高效的服务，赢得国内外市场的认可。 The company has maintained long-term in-depth cooperation with world-leading enterprises, including Caterpillar, SJPETRO, BELAZ, Cummins, Baudouin and STAMFORD, and has earned recognition from domestic and overseas markets for stable quality and efficient services.
